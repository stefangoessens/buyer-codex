# Core Primitives

Shared primitives required by `KIN-945`.

## Marketing Primitives

1. `SectionShell`
   - Wide desktop container, generous vertical rhythm, no ornamental clutter.
2. `EstimatorInput`
   - Single-line entry plus dominant CTA.
3. `ProofStat`
   - Number-first, short descriptor, optional caption.
4. `PropertyRail`
   - Horizontal rail that can collapse to a stacked mobile column.
5. `StepChip`
   - Number chip plus step title/body.
6. `TrustRow`
   - Trustpilot/review/logo strip with a single high-signal number.
7. `FAQAccordion`
   - Compact question rows and short answers, no essay-length body copy.

## Product Primitives

1. `AppCard`
   - White card, large radius, thin ring, medium shadow.
2. `MetricHeader`
   - Small label, dominant number, muted delta or qualifier.
3. `ChartFrame`
   - Quiet border and surface with a simple title/subtitle row.
4. `DenseFormSection`
   - Group related fields into a single card with 16-24px spacing.
5. `InlinePillButton`
   - Pill action button for app surfaces.

## Provisional Primitives

1. `ScoreBadge`
   - Hold until RealAdvisor is accessible.
2. `SideNavShell`
   - Hold until the correct shadcn shell view is visible in the preset preview.
3. `SearchGridShell`
   - Hold until RealAdvisor or the expected shadcn shell view is available.
